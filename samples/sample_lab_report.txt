Title: Esterification Lab Report

Abstract

Ethyl acetate was prepared from ethanol and acetic acid using sulfuric acid as the catalyst. The final product was isolated after washing and drying.

Experimental

Ethanol, acetic acid, and concentrated sulfuric acid were combined in a round-bottom flask. The reaction mixture was heated at reflux for 45 minutes, cooled, and transferred to a separatory funnel. The organic layer was washed with sodium bicarbonate solution and brine.

Results

The product mass was 3.21 g. The observed boiling range was 74-78 C. The isolated yield was calculated as 68%.

Conclusion

The experiment produced ethyl acetate in moderate yield. Further drying time may improve the measured purity.
